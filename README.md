GAME DESIGN DOCUMENT

1. Overview

 - Title: Malproksime
 - Genre: Walking Simulator
 - Summary: Short project based on the indi survival-horror game "Signalis" made by RoseEngine. 

2. Details

 - Game Mechanics: Movement, interaction with the environment and NPCs.
 - Controls: Standart mouse and keyboard.
 - Genre: Dystopia
 - Setting: Retro-futuristic totalitarian state.
 - Location: Mandelbrot Polytechnic High School.
 - Characters: Ariane Yeong, the main character. Isa and Erika Itou. Unnamed students and Replikas.

3. Story and the world.

 The player is offered a glimpse into Ariane's (one of Signalis's main characters) life. To be exact, it's the part where she moved to the unnamed city of Rotfront (planet) away from her mother to study. Since she had never left the Interplanetary station where she and her mother lived alone, moving to a big city and society is quite difficult. 
 
  Players can interact with and inspect various parts of the environment to uncover information and trivia about the location and the game's world as a whole. 
  
 The game is set in a Retro-Futuristic past, approximately 80-s. Humanity built its way to the other planets of the solar System. The solar system is under the control of two totalitarian states, between which a conflict has broken out and is currently ongoing for an unknown period of time. 

4. Audio and visuals

  -The main goal is to recreate the atmosphere of the original game. 
 
  -Visuals: Are most precisely described by "PS 1 graphics", which is quite popular nowadays. Low poly with lots of visual clutter, pixelized image and textures.
 
  -Music: Industrial Ambient, Dark Ambient, Ambient.
